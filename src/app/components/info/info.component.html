Welcome to PeeMap

Introduction

This guide will walk you through the features of our app, ensuring you can easily find and rate toilets, or even add new ones.

1. Adding a New Toilet

1.1 For Computer Users:

  1. Double-click anywhere on the screen.
  2. A dialog will pop up prompting you to specify the location of the new toilet.
  3. Enter details about where the toilet is located, e.g., "Inside Joe's Restaurant" or "At Central Train Station".
  4. Click the "Submit" button to add the toilet to the map.

1.2 For Mobile Phone Users:

  1. Hold your finger on the screen for a few seconds.
  2. A dialog will appear asking you to provide details about the toilet's location.
  3. Input the location details.
  4. Tap "Submit" to add the toilet to the map.

2. Finding the Nearest Toilet

  1. Look for the red button on the screen.
  2. Press the red button.
  3. The app will provide directions to the nearest toilet.

3. Rating a Toilet's Cleanliness

  1. Find the toilet you want to rate on the map.
  2. Click on the toilet's icon.
  3. A popup will appear, allowing you to rate the cleanliness of the toilet.
  4. Submit your rating.

Conclusion

Thank you for using the PeeMap Application! We hope this guide helps you navigate the app with ease. Your contributions in adding and rating toilets help make this community-driven tool more valuable for everyone. Safe travels and happy toilet finding!
